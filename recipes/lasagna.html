Lasagna

Description

Enjoy the classic italian dish. All you've gotta do is gather the Ingredients and follow the next Steps

Ingredients

  • 250gr of Lasagna pasta sheets
  • 500gr of marinara sauce
  • 400gr of ground beef
  • 200gr of mozarella cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven up to 180 celsius
  2. Mix buttter and wheat flour in a pot
  3. Add milk into the mix and stirr
  4. Mix the beef into the sauce
  5. Place the mix into a sheet of pasta and repeat
